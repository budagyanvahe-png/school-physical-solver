# school-physical-solver
It is physical calculator for school students to immediately calculate formulas for quick solving tasks in lessons.

### Problem
Student has NO TIME for calculations in school physical task, but he NEEDS get good mark. Also he may not know theory of physics. 

### Solution
Create the code to make calculations immediately and accurate. Student just needs enter numbers and he will get the correct answer and if this student is initiative, he can follow the link and learn more physics. 

### Target Audience (TA)
Student who need quickly calculate some physics quantity for solving school tasks and also wants updrade physics knowledge

### Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
Physical Solver proposes the student economy of time, efficiency of calculations and education origins
